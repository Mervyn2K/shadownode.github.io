Earn tokens by voting here

Why Vote

  • Voting increases our rank and reputation on voting websites. This can result in new players joining
  • The tokens you get from voting are a global form of currency which you can trade in for rewards.
  • We have a monthly giveaway of Steam Game Keys for permanent copies of certain games for the Top 3 Voters!
  • More details can be found on our Wiki entry about voting and rewards.

The Voting System

  • We have 2 voting website we are listed on. Each of which you can use to vote on, once per day.
  • You vote by filling in your Minecraft username in the text window and completing the Captcha.
  • Voting gives you 100 Tokens per voting website, which can be used for in-game items or currency.
